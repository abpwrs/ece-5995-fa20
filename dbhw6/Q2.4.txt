Get all the movies released in 2000 with the genres of Comedy or Romance and with title
that starts with M (include the png of the resulting graph including movie and genre nodes).

MATCH   (m:Movie)
WHERE   m.year = 2000 AND (m.title STARTS WITH 'M') AND ((m)-[:IN_GENRE]-(:Genre {name: 'Comedy'}) OR (m)-[:IN_GENRE]-(:Genre {name:'Romance'}))
RETURN  m;
